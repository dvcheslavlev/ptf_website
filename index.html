Value

  • With our 2D and 3D virtual advertisement technology we open up new opportunities in advertisement targeting and placement Our ad replacement technology allows for seamless integration of static and animated ads into the fight scene. This way broadcasters can show different advertisements to different viewers or sell more places for advertisements. Moreover, animated advertisements are more catchy and should have higher conversion rates. Finally, more creative 3D advertisements may actually improve user experience making the fight show more enjoyable
  • Our approach to tracking camera have minimal equipment requirements, it is the only feasible approach for martial sports we are currently aware of The existing approaches to visual effects are either very time consuming and do not work in real time, or require very expensive cameras and special equipment to track camera movements. Our approach does not require installation of new cameras or attaching any equipment to the cameras. All we need is access to existing cameras, physical measurements of the cage and print-outs of logos on canvas and cage walls
  • Our technology can make MMA broadcasts more exciting and engaging with wide range of visual effects and game-like 2D and 3D animations MMA fight is most importantly a show and its main goal is to entertain the audience. With our visual effects, 3D infographics and more creative advertisement, the viewer experience will definitely become more exciting. An improvement in viewer experience would attract more audience and would increase sales for existing followers
  • We make our software aiming to reduce the pain of integrating developers and designers work.
    We build on popular software tools among designers such as Blender and Unity, thus reducing overhead required to connect designer and developers work. We work to make sure that our software can take ready animations from clients as an input and output results as close as possible to designer`s imagination
  • We work closely with computer vision experts from OpenDataScience community and benefit from community support.
    Particularly we benefit from expert advice and volunteer work of community members, which include Kaggle Grandmasters and employees of top research labs and tech startups. We help volunteers to get practical knowledge of Computer Vision and Virtual Reality, while they provide us support with our projects. We would never get this far without their participation
  • Our research team with applied Data Science experience in finance, retail and tech industries continuously works on new features and products
  • Our main goal is full 3D reconstruction of MMA fights in Virtual Reality and our researchers work hard to bring us closer to achieving it. Current research focuses heavily on pose tracking and reconstruction of 3D models of fighters. With every break-through in our research, we would be able to create more products and features, creating more value for our investors and clients
